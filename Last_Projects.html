Project

The Right Skin

SEPTEMBER 28-29, 2019

Attended a 24 hour hackathon where I, along with two other teamates, made a website to inform people of the dangers of skin care products and how to choose the correct brands based on certain facial needs one may have, while also taking into consideration the enviormental impact of these products.

During this project the main thing I learned was how to work succesfully in a tight window of time. We could not prepare any information prior to the hackathon, meaning we had 24 hours to think of a project, make the website, and make a presentation to present to the rest of the people there. This mean we had to act fast and practically in order to suceed.

The judges had very good things to say about our project, such as how at the time they thought "this is a very creative idea, since there are not many websites that compile information of all different types of skin products", additionally noting how they "liked how your project focused on health and wellness but also incorporated environmental impact".

Our code contained around 138 lines of HTML and javascript combined, along with 270 lines of CSS
